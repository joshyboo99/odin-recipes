Clapshot

Clapshot is a Scottish vegetable dish that‘s traditionally served as an accompaniment to haggis, beef, pork, lamb, or game.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound baking potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3/4 pound turnips, peeled and cubed
  • Â¼ pound carrots, peeled and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream

Steps

  1. Place potatoes, turnips, carrots, and salt into a Dutch oven. Fill with water to cover vegetables and bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce the heat to medium-high and boil until vegetables are tender, 20 to 25 minutes.
  2. Drain and mash vegetables with a potato masher. Spoon mashed vegetables into a serving dish. Top with butter and drizzle with cream.
